700 MW ZUNGERU HYDROELECTRIC POWER PROJECT

The 700MW Zungeru Hydro-Electric Power Project is located on the Kaduna River in the State of Niger, approximately 150km at crow flight North West of Abuja, the Federal Capital of the Republic of Nigeria.
It is designed to generate 2,630 GWh energy at reservoir FSL 230 and flow rate of 880m3/sec, total installed capacity of 700MW with a plant factor of 0.33.
